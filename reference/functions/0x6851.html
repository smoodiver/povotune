Summary

Main diagnostic housekeeping routine (0x6851–0x6B13). Runs decay timers for coolant temp correction, speed-dependent values, EGT variables. Manages IAC target tracking with slew-rate limiting. Runs prescaler for IAC table lookups. Processes TPS/load rate-of-change filtering. Updates numerous debounce counters. Manages warm-up timers.
